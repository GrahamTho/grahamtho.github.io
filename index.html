About Me

My name is Graham Thomas. I am 14 years old and a confused high school freshman and I love my hobbies. I grew up in San Marcos, Texas, But moved to North Carolina when I was ten because my mom went to graduate school at UNC. When this happened, I was upset to leave all of my friends, but it wasn't as bad as I thought it would be. At least, for a while it wasn't. During fifth grade, I descended into the darkest depression of my entire life. It got so bad that I was hospitalized more times than I can count. When I finally recovered, I was fourteen years old.


That's me right now. Happy, healthy, and sane. I am so thankful for my parents who have supported me through these hard times, and for my few friends who made life worth living. This is me.
